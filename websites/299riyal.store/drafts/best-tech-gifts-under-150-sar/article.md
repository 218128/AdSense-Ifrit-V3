# Best Tech Gifts Under 150 SAR for Friends and Family

Buying gifts is hard. Buying cheap gifts that don't *look* cheap is even harder.
If you have a budget of 150 SAR and want to impress your brother, sister, or colleague, skip the perfumes and chocolates. Give them gadget they will actually use every day.

## 1. The Gamer Gift: RGB Mousepad
**Item:** Havit RGB Gaming Mouse Pad
**Price:** ~60 SAR
It lights up their desk, makes their setup look professional, and costs next to nothing.
*   **Wow Factor:** 14 lighting modes.

## 2. The Driver Gift: Xiaomi Car Charger (Fast)
**Item:** Xiaomi 67W Car Charger
**Price:** ~99 SAR
For the friend who always has a dead phone. This isn't a normal charger; it charges a phone from 0 to 100% in 35 minutes while driving.
*   **Wow Factor:** Speed. It even charges laptops.

## 3. The Homebody Gift: Sunset Lamp
**Item:** Projection Sunset Lamp
**Price:** ~40-60 SAR
These went viral on TikTok. They project a warm, golden-hour circle of light on the wall. Perfect for making a room look cozy or for taking selfies.
*   **Wow Factor:** Vibes.

## 4. The Practical Gift: Anker 323 Power Bank (10,000mAh)
**Item:** Anker PowerCore
**Price:** ~129 SAR
Everyone needs extra power. Giving an Anker device shows you care about quality.
*   **Wow Factor:** Utility. They will use this for years.

## 5. The Fun Gift: Mini Portable Karaoke Mic
**Item:** WSTER Wireless Microphone
**Price:** ~35 SAR
It's a microphone with a speaker built-in. Connect it to a phone and start singing. Guaranteed laughs at family gatherings.
*   **Wow Factor:** Fun.

## Conclusion
You don't need to buy an iPad to be a great gift-giver. Focus on things that solve a problem (Charging) or bring joy (Lights/Music).
